Quiz

What is the difference between var and let?
You can reassign values and redeclare with 'var' while with 'let' can only be reassigned.

What is the difference between var and const?

You can reassign and redeclare with 'var' but with 'const' you can't reassign or redeclare.

What is the difference between let and const?

You can reassign with 'let' but 'const' doesn't let you reassign or redeclare.

What is hoisting?

Hoisting is the manner in which variable names and values are accessed when they are needed. They are 'hoisted' to the beginning of a given scope to be accessed later.
